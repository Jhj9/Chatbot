Description

We created a chatbot demo to utilize information related to the bridge. Users can submit a query about the bridge to receive a response from the chatbot. The chatbot extracts context and answers from the Bridge Manual to formulate its response. You can also check the source of the context utilized to formulate the response and the content of the corresponding document.
